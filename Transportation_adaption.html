Transportation and Adaptation

Introduction

Transportation and adaptation are part of everyday life. Climate change affects how transport systems work and how living beings survive in different environments.

Transportation

Transportation is affected by climate change. Extreme heat damages roads and railway tracks. Heavy rain and floods disrupt traffic and damage infrastructure. Storms and rising sea levels affect ports and airports, especially in coastal areas.

  • Heat damages roads and rail tracks
  • Floods disrupt transport movement
  • Storms affect ports and airports
Adaptation

Adaptation means making changes to deal with environmental problems. Transport systems are improved using stronger materials, better drainage, and better planning to reduce damage during extreme weather.

Conclusion

Transportation systems and living beings both need adaptation to handle environmental changes.
